The theater

Opéra Zitto welcomes you to an idyllic and historic location: the Taormina Theatre. A true ancient heritage, it is one of Sicily's most prized ruins due to its remarkable preservation and the beauty of its location. This is why we invite you to respect this place by following the few safety instructions.

Security

Dear customers, here is a list of safety instructions and prohibited items on the festival site.

Instructions

  • Do not throw waste on the ground, garbage cans are available on the site.
  • The consumption of tobacco is prohibited outside the air provided for this purpose.
  • The distribution of flyers, or any type of promotion, without authorization is prohibited.
  • Children under 12 are not allowed.
  • Animals are prohibited on the site.

Forbidden objects

  • Illegal substances and alcohol not coming from outside the festival.
  • Blunt or sharp object or firearms (even dummy).
  • Drink or food not coming from the festival.
  • Any type of aerosol.
  • Drone, megaphone, laser pointer or any other object that may disturb the representations.
  • Big bags.

Health

Dear customers, here is a list of barrier gestures that we recommend to preserve your hearing while fully enjoying the festival.

  • Do not stand in front of the speakers.
  • Take breaks and isolate yourself from noise for a few moments.
  • Do not hesitate to use equipment such as earplugs (earplugs of different sizes will be available at the ticket checkpoint.
  • Keep young and old people away from speakers.

Accéssibility

Opera Zitto is accessible to everyone. Devices have been installed to accommodate as many people as possible.

  • Disabled access to access the stage.
  • Adapted points of sale.
  • Toilets accessible to people with reduced mobility.
  • Staff trained in sign language.
  • All our information is available in French, English and Italian.
